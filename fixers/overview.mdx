## What is a Fixer?

A **Fixer** is a service professional on the Servineo platform who offers skilled services to customers. Fixers can create job offers, manage appointments, handle payments, and build their professional reputation.

<Note>
Fixers must complete a registration process and profile setup before they can start receiving job requests.
</Note>

## Profile Information

Your fixer profile includes:

- **Contact Details**: Email, phone number, and ID (CI)
- **Services**: Categories of services you offer (Plumbing, Electrical, Carpentry, etc.)
- **Payment Methods**: Accepted payment types (Cash, QR, Credit Card)
- **Vehicle**: Whether you have your own transportation
- **Location**: Your work area and service radius
- **Portfolio**: Photos and descriptions of completed work
- **Certifications**: Professional credentials and qualifications
- **Experience**: Work history and expertise

<Tip>
Complete your profile 100% to increase visibility and trust with potential customers. A complete profile shows a green indicator badge.
</Tip>
