## What is a Requester?

As a requester in Servineo, you can search for professional service providers (fixers), book appointments, manage payments, and rate services. The platform connects you with skilled professionals across various categories.

## Key Features

### Advanced Search & Filtering

Find the perfect fixer for your needs using our comprehensive search system:

- **Category Filters**: Browse by service tags and categories
- **Location-based**: Filter by city and proximity
- **Rating System**: View fixers rated from 1.0 to 5.0 stars
- **Price Ranges**: Find services within your budget
- **Date Availability**: Check fixer availability before booking

### Appointment Management

Book and manage appointments seamlessly:

- **Virtual or In-Person**: Choose your preferred appointment type
- **Real-time Availability**: See available time slots instantly
- **Appointment Tracking**: Monitor your upcoming and past appointments
- **Rescheduling**: Modify appointments when needed

### Secure Payments

Multiple payment options for your convenience:

- QR code payments
- Digital wallet integration
- Transaction history
- Invoice management

### Rating System

Help build trust in the community:

- Rate fixers from 1 to 3 stars
- Leave detailed comments
- View ratings from other requesters

## User Profile

Your requester profile contains:

- **Personal Information**: Name, email, and phone number
- **Location**: Your registered address and coordinates
- **Appointment History**: Past and upcoming appointments
- **Payment Methods**: Saved payment preferences
- **Security**: Two-factor authentication support via Google Authenticator

<Note>
  Keep your contact information up-to-date to ensure fixers can reach you for appointment confirmations.
</Note>
